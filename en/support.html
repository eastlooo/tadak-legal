Quick Summary

  • Support, legal questions, and account deletion questions are handled through the official email.
  • Email requests are reviewed and answered in order on business days.
  • If you need account deletion, first review the in-app deletion route.

1. Support Channel

Use this channel for app support, bug reports, account deletion questions, and public legal document inquiries.

2. Helpful Information to Include

  • Platform in use: iOS or Android
  • App version and device model
  • Screen, reproduction steps, and error message
  • For account issues, the login email or other identifying details

3. Account Deletion Support

  • If you can access the app, use the in-app path: `Profile > Account Management > Delete`.
  • If you cannot access the app, you may email the support address for deletion-related assistance.
  • The current deletion scope and handling flow are described on the Account Deletion page.

4. Operating Standard

  • Email requests are processed in order on business days.
  • Account deletion and privacy-related inquiries use the same email channel.
  • Billing and refund handling also follows the applicable app store policy.
