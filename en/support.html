Tadak Support

Support and contact information for Tadak

Published: 2026-03-06 Primary Contact: omilsoft@gmail.com

Quick Summary

  • Support, legal inquiries, account deletion requests, and moderation appeals all use the same official email.
  • If you can access the app, please use the in-app deletion route first for account deletion.
  • Questions about store billing, ad-removal restore, post or user reports, and hidden-author controls are also handled through the same channel.

1. Support Channel

Email

omilsoft@gmail.com

Use this address for app support, bug reports, privacy requests, account deletion help, moderation appeals, and store billing or restore questions.

2. What to Include in Your Request

  • For account deletion: whether you can still access the app and what login method you used
  • For moderation or reports: account-identifying details, whether it was a post report or user report, post title or report timing, and your explanation
  • For ad removal purchase or restore: platform, store account state, purchase or restore timing, and any error message
  • For privacy requests: login method, platform, and the specific request

3. Operating Standard

  • Email requests are reviewed on business days in the order received.
  • Account deletion, privacy, moderation, and billing inquiries all use the single contact address: `omilsoft@gmail.com`.
  • Store billing, refunds, and restore outcomes themselves also follow Apple App Store or Google Play policy.
