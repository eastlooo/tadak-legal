Tadak Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect personal information

Effective: 2026-02-23 Last Updated: 2026-02-23

Quick Summary

  • We process personal information only for service and security purposes.
  • We do not sell personal information without legal basis.
  • You may request access, correction, deletion and suspension.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account/authentication data: user identifiers, login provider, email (if provided).
  • Profile data: nickname and app preference settings.
  • Usage/device data: logs, events, errors, app version and OS information.
  • Billing/ad data: purchase status and ad events within permitted scope.

2. Purpose of Use

  • Provide and operate the service and account functions.
  • Support record sync and user support flows.
  • Improve quality, prevent abuse and respond to incidents.
  • Process payments and analytics for operations.

3. Retention Period

  • We delete personal data once processing purposes are met.
  • Data may be retained where required by law.

4. Third-Party Processors

We may rely on external service providers for operations.

  • Infrastructure/Data: Supabase
  • Analytics/Logging: Firebase Analytics and related tools
  • Ads: Google AdMob and related networks
  • Authentication: Apple/Google sign-in providers

We do not sell personal information without legal basis.

5. International Processing

Data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country due to cloud and analytics infrastructure.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access, correction, deletion and suspension of processing.
  • You may contact us regarding account unlinking and withdrawal support.

7. Security Measures

  • We operate technical and administrative safeguards for data protection.
  • We follow incident response procedures to reduce impact and prevent recurrence.

8. Policy Changes

We may revise this policy and will provide notice for material updates.

9. Privacy Officer

  • Team: OMILSOFT Privacy Team
  • Email: omilsoft@gmail.com
  • Request handling: processed in order on business days

For privacy inquiries: omilsoft@gmail.com
