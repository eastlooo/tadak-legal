Tadak Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, retain, and delete personal information

Effective: 2026-03-06 Last Updated: 2026-03-06

Quick Summary

  • We process personal information only for service operation, account management, and security.
  • You can exercise your rights through the in-app account deletion flow or by contacting us by email.
  • For current deletion targets, the service deletes data rather than retaining it in anonymized form.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account/authentication data: user identifiers, login provider details, email if provided.
  • Profile data: nickname and app settings such as theme, font, and language.
  • Usage/device data: logs, feature events, error logs, app version, and OS information.
  • Billing/ad data: purchase state and ad events within the permitted scope.
  • Community activity data: posts, likes, imports, and report history.

2. Purposes of Use

  • Provide typing practice, progress storage, account management, and community features.
  • Handle sync, user support, and account deletion requests.
  • Improve quality, respond to incidents, and prevent abuse or policy violations.
  • Process payments, analytics, and operational decision making.

3. Retention and Deletion

  • We delete data without undue delay once the processing purpose is fulfilled.
  • Data may be retained only for the period required by law.
  • When account deletion is completed, the current service deletes profile, records, user-created posts, likes, imports, reports, and related audit records.
  • For these current deletion targets, we do not operate a separate anonymized retention policy.

4. External Services

We may rely on the following external services to operate the app.

  • Infrastructure/Data: Supabase
  • Analytics/Logging: Firebase Analytics and related tools
  • Ads: Google AdMob and related ad services
  • Authentication: login providers supported by the app
  • Billing: app store payment systems such as the iOS App Store or Android Google Play

We do not sell personal information without a legal basis.

5. International Processing

Personal information may be processed outside your country because of cloud infrastructure and analytics tooling. We apply legally required safeguards.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access, correction, deletion, and suspension of processing.
  • You may use the in-app deletion flow or email us if you cannot access the app.
  • Detailed deletion scope and handling steps are available on the Account Deletion page.

7. Security Measures

  • We operate technical and administrative safeguards for access control, transmission protection, and monitoring.
  • We follow incident response procedures to reduce impact and prevent recurrence.

8. Policy Changes

We may revise this policy and will provide notice for material changes through public links or in-app notice.

9. Privacy Contact

  • Channel: OMILSOFT Privacy Team
  • Email: omilsoft@gmail.com
  • Email requests are reviewed and answered in order on business days.

10. External Relief Channels (Korea)

If you need legal or regulatory assistance in Korea, you may contact the following agencies.

  • Personal Information Infringement Report Center: privacy.kisa.or.kr
  • Cyber Investigation Division (Supreme Prosecutors' Office): cybercid.spo.go.kr
  • Korean National Police Cyber Bureau: ecrm.police.go.kr
