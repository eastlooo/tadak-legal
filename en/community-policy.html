Tadak Community Policy

Operating standards for the public community feed

Published: 2026-03-06 Updated: 2026-03-06

Quick Summary

  • The public community feed is used to share typing-practice content.
  • Profanity, abusive language, sexually explicit content, illegal content, and rights infringement are prohibited.
  • Posts with repeated reports may be temporarily hidden when required for service operations.

1. Scope

  • This policy applies to Tadak's public community area.
  • Community sharing availability may depend on the account-linking state supported by the app.

2. Basic Posting Standard

  • Shared text should be suitable for typing practice.
  • Guest usage may be restricted from public sharing, and the app may require account linking before upload.
  • You must post only content that does not infringe the rights of others.

3. Prohibited Content

  • Profanity, harassment, abuse, humiliation, or hateful expression
  • Sexual, explicit, or pornographic content
  • Illegal content, copyright infringement, impersonation, or deceptive content
  • Spam, repeated flooding, unauthorized automation, or other interference with service operations

4. Reports and Visibility Restrictions

  • Users can submit reports on public community posts.
  • Posts with repeated reports may be temporarily hidden when operational review requires it.
  • If a policy violation is confirmed, the service may remove the post, limit sharing, or apply other required operating measures.

5. Contact

Community policy questions: omilsoft@gmail.com
