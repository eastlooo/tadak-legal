Tadak Account Deletion

How to delete your Tadak account and app data

Published: 2026-03-06 Standalone URL

Quick Summary

  • You can delete your account directly in the app through `Profile > Account Management > Delete`.
  • Once confirmed, the current service deletes profile data, records, user-created posts, and related activity history.
  • If you cannot access the app, contact omilsoft@gmail.com.

1. How to Request Deletion

  1. Open the Tadak app.
  2. Go to `Profile > Account Management`.
  3. Select `Delete`.
  4. Acknowledge the notice and confirm `Delete Account`.

If you cannot access the app, you may email the support address for deletion-related assistance.

2. Deletion and Anonymization Scope

  • Deleted items: profile, practice records, user-created posts, public community posts, likes, imports, reports, and related audit records.
  • For guest usage: the guest account, local records, and created posts are removed together.
  • For these current targets, the service deletes the data rather than retaining it in anonymized form.

3. Handling Flow and SLA

  • Once final in-app confirmation is completed, deletion is executed immediately and cannot be reversed.
  • Email requests are reviewed and answered in order on business days.
  • If identity verification is required, we may ask for additional confirmation by email.

4. Notes

  • Tadak account deletion covers the service account and app data managed by OMILSOFT.
  • Deletion or closure of external login provider accounts is governed by each provider's own policy.
