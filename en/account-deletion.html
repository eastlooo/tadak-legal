Tadak Account Deletion

How to delete your Tadak account and app data

Published: 2026-03-06 Standalone URL

Quick Summary

  • You can delete your account directly in the app through `Profile > Account Management > Delete`.
  • Once confirmed, account-linked data such as profile information, records, writings, likes, imports, and post-report history is handled as deletion targets.
  • tracking, feedback, ad, ATT, review, and user-report logs may be retained for operational purposes only after account identifiers are removed.

1. How to Request Deletion

  1. Open the Tadak app.
  2. Go to `Profile > Account Management`.
  3. Select `Delete`.
  4. Acknowledge the notice and confirm `Delete Account`.

If you cannot access the app, email omilsoft@gmail.com for deletion support.

2. Data Deleted with the Account

  • Profile data, nickname, and settings
  • Practice records and rank-related data
  • Private writings and public community posts you created
  • Likes, imports, and post-report history tied to your account
  • Your hidden or blocked author list
  • Certain account-linked audit records such as legacy migration audit entries

3. Data That May Remain After Deletion

  • tracking_events may remain only after user_id, anonymous_id, session_id, and other account identifiers are removed and identifier fields inside payload are redacted.
  • In-app feedback and bug reports may remain only after user_id is removed, the message is replaced with a deleted-account placeholder, and identifier fields in metadata are redacted.
  • ad_interstitial_events, att_prompt_events, and review_funnel_events may remain only after user_id and identifier fields in metadata are removed.
  • community_user_reports may remain as moderation history only after reporter or target identifiers are removed and identifying fields inside the target snapshot are redacted.
  • Apple App Store or Google Play purchase, restore, or refund history is retained separately by the relevant store provider.
  • Your Google, Apple, or Kakao account with that provider is not automatically closed or deleted when you delete your Tadak account.

4. Guest Account Deletion

  • Deleting a guest account removes the guest state together with writings and records tied to that guest account.
  • Guest accounts cannot publish publicly in the community feed. Public sharing requires account linking first.

5. Handling Flow and Important Notes

  • After final confirmation in the app, deletion is executed immediately and cannot be reversed.
  • Email requests are reviewed on business days in the order received.
  • We may request additional information to verify identity or confirm the requested scope.
